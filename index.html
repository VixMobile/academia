About Us

Our Main Focus -
Postnatal Pilates

Pilates With Purpose was created to help postnatal mothers feel stronger and healthier again. As our tagline suggests, we'll work together to get back to the true you!

Many women suffer from:
- Abdominal separation
- Caesarean section recovery
- Incontinence
- Poor posture from breast/bottle feeding
- Tired muscles from holding/rocking the baby
- Sacroiliac joint (SIJ) movement and pain
- Low energy levels
- Poor self-esteem regarding body image

We know that after you've had a baby it can be tough to get out of the house - especially to group exercise classes. And if you've returned to work, we understand it can be even harder to find the time, so we offer convenient home visits.

We'll help you to:
- Strength your core and pelvic floor muscles
- Feel stronger from head to toe
- Stretch those tight spots
- Enhance flexibility
- Increase your body awareness
- Improve your posture

We want to save you:
- Child minding fees
- Hassles of travelling
- Time
- Cost of a gym membership
- Feeling self-conscious at the gym

Our instructors at Pilates With Purpose focus on giving you exercises that help you function more easily in everyday life. We take pride in seeing you achieve results.

Other Focuses

Rehabilitation
& Post Operative

Whether you've had a hip replacement and want to get strong enough to climb the stairs in your own home, or you are recovering from a lower back injury and need to strengthen your abdominal muscles to help support the spine, we can tailor exercises specifically for your needs and help you achieve your recovery goals.

Functional Living
& Injury Prevention

Our instructors at Pilates With Purpose focus on giving you exercises that help you function more easily in everyday life. By strengthening your muscles, increasing your mobility and flexibility, and improving your posture, you can be less likely to injure yourself. We take pride in seeing you achieve results.

Corporate
Group Classes

We offer group pilates classes at your workplace, and recommend a minimum of 6 participants and a maximum of 15. Pilates at your workplace can be a great way to break up or finish the day, is a fantastic opportunity for employers to reward staff and create a positive team building environment. Please contact us for more information.
